How It Works

Three steps to multiplayer

No manual configuration. No IP addresses. No ROM matching. Outbreak handles everything.

01

Pick a Game

Browse Outbreak Host — a curated collection of 740+ multiplayer-ready games. Highlight a title and hold A to host.

02

Instant Discovery

Your session appears on every Outbreak machine on the network and across the internet. No IPs to type, no lobby codes.

03

One-Button Join

The other player sees your session, presses A, and they're in. ROMs, cores, and settings are matched automatically.
